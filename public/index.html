⎈ Fun while learning distributed systems

Hands-on simulators for the systems you usually only read about — break them, watch them heal, understand them. Pick your playground:
🌍

Shared Cluster

One live simulation for everyone on the internet, served by the backend. Kill a pod and the whole world sees it die. Expect chaos from strangers — that's the fun. May be slow (or down!) under heavy load, by design.

Enter the shared cluster →
🧍

Solo Sandbox

Your own private cluster, running 100% in your browser — same engine, zero server involvement. Nobody can touch your pods, it works at any speed, and it never goes down. Best for focused, distraction-free practice.

Launch your own sandbox →
🚨

Incident Room

A hidden fault is injected into a Kubernetes cluster and users are complaining. Read the events, logs and charts, diagnose the root cause, apply the fix and watch it heal. How long is your streak?

Take the on-call shift →
📨

Kafka Lag Lab

Partitions, consumer groups and the physics of lag. Create a hot key and watch one partition drown, trigger rebalancing storms, kill a broker mid-stream — all in your browser.

Start producing →
🌩

Retry Storm

A three-service chain where a slow database plus naive retries equals a self-inflicted outage. Tune timeouts, backoff and circuit breakers to ride out the storm — or cause one on purpose.

Brew a storm →
